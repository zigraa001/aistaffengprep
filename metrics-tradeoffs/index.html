Metrics, Tradeoffs & Engineering Impact

Master the staff+ skill of measuring success, reasoning about tradeoffs, and connecting technical decisions to business outcomes. Move from "I built it" to "I can prove it mattered."

Course Progress 0% Complete

Course Philosophy

  • Second-order thinking first — Every metric has consequences beyond what you intend to measure
  • Business impact always — Technical metrics only matter when connected to outcomes
  • Q&A format — Content presented as questions with collapsible answers for active recall
  • Real numbers, real systems — No hand-waving; actual data from Netflix, Google, Uber, Stripe

Who This Course Is For

  • Staff and principal engineers who need to quantify the impact of their technical decisions
  • Senior engineers preparing to demonstrate business-aware technical leadership in interviews
  • Tech leads who want to build a metrics-driven engineering culture
  • Anyone who's been told "you need to show more impact" in their promo packet

Key Themes Throughout

Critical Thinking

Every module challenges you to think about what could go wrong, what second-order effects exist, and what you might be missing.

Business Connection

Technical metrics are always tied back to revenue, user satisfaction, cost, and competitive advantage.

Real Numbers

No vague "it depends" — concrete data points, formulas, and benchmarks from real production systems.

Q&A Active Recall

Content presented as questions with collapsible answers. Try to answer before revealing — this is how you build interview readiness.
