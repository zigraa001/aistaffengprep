Module 6: Culture & Value Alignment

Learning Objectives

  • Map your stories to company values
  • Adapt communication style
  • Demonstrate authentic alignment
  • Ask insightful questions

Company Value Quick Reference

  • Move with urgency and focus
  • Think rigorously
  • Trust and amplify
  • Global optimization

Netflix

  • Freedom & responsibility
  • Independent judgment
  • Radical candor
  • Keeper test mentality
  • Context, not control

OpenAI

  • AGI focus -- building broadly beneficial AI

Value-Story Mapping

How to Map Stories ▼
  1. Identify values: List 3-5 core company values
  2. Review your stories: Which demonstrate each value?
  3. Adapt framing: Same story, different emphasis
  4. Use their language: Mirror company terminology

Staff-Level Tip

At the staff level, your stories should emphasize cross-team impact, technical strategy, and influencing without authority -- not just individual contribution. Frame every story around the organizational outcome, not just the technical solution.

Authenticity Matters

Key Principle

Don't fake alignment. If your values don't match, it's better for both sides. Focus on:

  • Finding genuine connections
  • Being honest about what excites you
  • Asking about areas you want to learn more

Questions to Ask

Company-Specific Questions ▼

For Stripe:

  • "How does the team balance API stability with iteration speed?"
  • "What's a recent decision where 'users first' created an interesting trade-off?"

For Netflix:

  • "Can you share an example of 'freedom and responsibility' in practice?"
  • "How does the team handle disagreements on technical direction?"

For Anthropic:

  • "How does safety consideration affect day-to-day engineering decisions?"
  • "What's an example of a project where you had to balance speed with safety?"
  • "How do staff engineers influence research direction here?"

For OpenAI:

  • "How has the engineering culture evolved over the past year?"
  • "What does the staff engineer role look like in terms of scope -- team-level or org-level?"

For Databricks/Snowflake:

  • "How do staff engineers balance customer-specific requests with platform-wide architectural decisions?"
  • "What role does open source play in the team's engineering strategy?" (Databricks)
  • "How do you approach performance optimization trade-offs at scale?" (Snowflake)
